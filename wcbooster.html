Webcam booster shim thingy

I have had a bit of a problem. My webcam front fin is long enough it hangs past the bevel section of my monitor. It leaves an iPhone-esque notch pattern blocking the view. I have put up with this for years and decided to stick something underneith it to raise it up out of the way (vs cutting the fin down).

Picture of the problem

Much to my surprise, I didnt have anything of the appropriate size to simply stick under it. So of course, I measured it.

Measurements:

  • top of the monitor width: 15mm
  • webcam landing area: 45mmx50mm deep.
  • overhang: 5mm for my monitor bezel
so a 40x10x5mm (wxdxh) rectangle should do the trick. 20 minutes creating a rectangle with two constraints in FreeCAD, which would have taken someone who's used the software before 0.1 seconds. Extremely advanced stuff here!

Ultra extreme FreeCAD design
Then I sliced it..
and printed it..

Took a whopping 8 minutes as you could imagine.

I love this ultra cheap ($200 at the time) FLsun Q5 delta printer

Success

Sometimes it feels good to overkill fix the small stuff
